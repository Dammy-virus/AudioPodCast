Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: January 2025

How to Delete Your Data

You can request deletion of your personal data from AudioCast through the following methods:

Method 1: Through Your Account

  1. Log in to your AudioCast account
  2. Go to Account Settings
  3. Click on "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm your decision

Method 2: Contact Support

If you're unable to delete your data through your account, you can:

  1. Email us at support@audiocast.com
  2. Include "Data Deletion Request" in the subject line
  3. Provide your account email address
  4. We will process your request within 30 days

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • Your profile information
  • Stream history and analytics
  • Account settings and preferences
  • Any stored audio content

Data Retention

Some information may be retained for legal, security, or technical purposes. This data will be fully anonymized and cannot be linked to your identity.
